## What This Application Does

AI-powered content analysis system that helps creators improve their content quality across multiple dimensions:
- Structure & Organization
- Tone & Voice  
- Accessibility & Readability
- Platform-Specific Optimization

Supports: Blog posts, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and Medium

---

## Deployment Overview

1. **Deploy Backend to Vercel** (~5 minutes)
2. **Set Up Vercel KV Database** (~2 minutes)
3. **Configure Environment Variables** (~3 minutes)
4. **Test Backend** (~2 minutes)
5. **Configure & Run Frontend** (~3 minutes)

**Total Time: ~15 minutes**
